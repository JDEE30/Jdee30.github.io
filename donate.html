Donate Now

Your monthly donation or one time donation will go a long way in helping those who can't help themselves. Your donation will go to feed children, elderly people and sick people in need of shelter, food or other supplies such as toiletries and basic things like clothes. Donate to us and we'll make it really easy to give back. You can write off your donation on your taxes and feel good about giving back to the less fortunate from the comfort of your own home. We distrubute funds to local houseless people in need and ensure your money goes to help out the less fortunate.







Covid 19

Covid19 has been a huge problem for millions of americans who were already struggling or who may have not been struggling before. We take necesary precautions when we make our deliveries by making sure our volunteers are using proper handwashing techniques, maintaining social distancing and wearing proper ppe such as surgical or n95 masks to ensure that we are only helping those in need and not causing any harm. This pandemic had made your donation even more necesary than before. There are so many americans out of work, or newly homeless despite our governments and local leaders best efforts. We're asking you to do your part in helping keep the most vulnerable in our society safe and healthy.
